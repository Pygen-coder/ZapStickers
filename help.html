Ordering & Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking link and number. You can also find your tracking information in the "My Orders" section of your profile.

Unfortunately, orders cannot be modified once placed. You can request a cancellation from your profile's "My Orders" section within 2 hours of placing the order. After that, cancellation is not possible as the order goes into production.
